Sushi Rolls

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes

Servings: 4 (32 pieces)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin strips
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 200g sushi-grade salmon or tuna, sliced
  • 4 imitation crab sticks, torn into strips
  • Soy sauce, for serving
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger, for serving

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sushi rice until the water runs clear. Cook the rice with 2 1/4 cups water in a rice cooker or on the stovetop.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Heat until sugar dissolves. Let cool.
  3. When the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture. Let cool to room temperature.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat.
  5. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch strip uncovered at the top edge.
  6. Arrange your chosen fillings in a line across the center of the rice.
  7. Using the mat, roll the sushi away from you, pressing gently to keep it tight.
  8. Moisten the uncovered edge of nori with water to seal the roll.
  9. Repeat with remaining nori sheets and fillings.
  10. Using a sharp knife, cut each roll into 8 pieces.
  11. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 310 per serving (8 pieces)

Protein: 12g

Carbohydrates: 52g

Fat: 7g
