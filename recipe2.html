Chicken Tikka Masala

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 800g boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, half of the garlic, half of the ginger, and half of each spice. Add chicken and marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add remaining garlic, ginger, and spices. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until no longer pink, about 8-10 minutes.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until sauce thickens.
  6. Pour in cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt to taste and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  8. Serve hot with rice and naan bread.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 450 per serving

Protein: 35g

Carbohydrates: 15g

Fat: 28g
